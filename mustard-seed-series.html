Mustard-Seed Seed Series (2021)

This series is part of a larger program of using an image as the seed for processing into new images. In this series the seed image was an image of a mustard plant. Each of these images represents one outcome of processing the former. Unfortunately, except for 'digital tapestry,' the original files for these works were lost, all that remains are images of the prints.
