Bio

Corey Thuro is a musician, writer, organizer, visual artist, dancer, and programmer living in Baltimore, MD. Despite being self taught in all of them He has sustained dedication and practice in each of these fields, and has focused much of his time to active participation in the Baltimore-DC experimental cultural scenes.
In 2019 he began a correspondence with the philosophers Henry Flynt and John Berndt which has contributed radically to the development of his intellectual activities.


---Professionally, I have presented workshops at venues such as the Kennedy Center and RhizomeDC. As a programmer I have worked front and back ends, explored data visualization, various libraries and frameworks, and has built audio processing and music machines in Max/MSP.
